Our Story

Hi. I’m Yevhen Davydenko.

I’m the founder of Davyway Inc. I like to call myself a guide who helps others to make the right choice while shopping online. The idea for my company name comes from my childhood. I have always been eager to find my own path in life and be useful to people.

In 2019 I got the ball rolling and with this in mind, I found that Amazon is the best e-commerce service provider to help me put my ambitious project into motion. My main goal is to bring consumers the highest-quality goods that they deserve.

Davy means «beloved» in Hebrew and serves as a part of my surname. Davyway stands for both my life direction and a beloved choice for customers.

By building my own way to success, I hope I can help others become a bit happier. When a customer is satisfied with their purchase, they are motivated and excited to move forward with their own dreams and aspirations.

I invite Amazon consumers to enjoy shopping with Davyway!

Sincerely yours,

Yevhen Davydenko
